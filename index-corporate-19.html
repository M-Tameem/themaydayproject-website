AI Assistant:

Mayday’s AI assistant orchestrates decision-making processes that elevate responsiveness to unprecedented levels. Mayday’s roBerta, BART, and Natural Language Processing analyze call transcripts accurately, extracting essential details and summarizing the emergency situation. Less Barriers to Aid

Using dynamic AI and natural language processing capabilities, Mayday will guarantee that no citizen in dire need will not get aid at the critical moment where they need it. Using Mayday, users will have the option to connect with Mayday's AI assistant, so that no one will be denied aid or put on hold due to volume of call or understaffed call centers. This is because Mayday's AI assistant will filter through the situations that require operator assistance the most, and send them to the caller.

How Mayday works

Our Vision: Revolutionizing Emergency Response Through AI Empowerment

An AI-integrated solution addresses delayed emergency response during peak times. Callers can interact with an AI assistant when operators are busy. Calls are accurately recorded and transcribed using Google's Speech-to-Text service.

Advanced AI technologies, including Google's Natural Language API and the RoBERTa model, analyze transcripts. Key details such as the essence and location of the emergency are extracted. Extracted data is securely stored in Firebase for real-time access by emergency teams.

Twilio's voice response capabilities ensure seamless transitions between AI and human operators. High caller satisfaction is maintained. The approach aims to improve emergency response efficiency by allowing human operators to focus on critical tasks while AI handles initial data processing.
